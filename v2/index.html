What we stand for

An employee promise built on six pillars.

Every role at Arabian Drilling sits on the same foundation. We commit to a safe and growing environment. In return, we expect commitment, discipline, and care for the people you work with.

01 — Pillar

Safety First

Safety is the foundation of every job, every site, and every decision we make.

02 — Pillar

Growth Through Experience

Real careers built through training, hands-on practice, mentoring, and operational exposure.

03 — Pillar

Technical Excellence

Disciplined, skilled people keep complex drilling operations running reliably.

04 — Pillar

Purpose & National Contribution

Support Saudi Arabia's energy sector and the development of local technical talent.

05 — Pillar

Teamwork & Connection

Performance depends on trust, communication, leadership, and real collaboration.

06 — Pillar

Wellbeing & Resilience

Drilling work demands focus and readiness. We support the discipline it takes to thrive.
