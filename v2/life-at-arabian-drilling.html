A culture built on STRIVE.

Our values

STRIVE — the six values that hold AD together.

S

Safety

Safety is the foundation of every job, every site, and every decision we make.

T

Teamwork

We rely on each other. Trust, communication, and care for the crew matter every shift.

R

Reliability

Show up, do the work, deliver against the standard. Reliability is the AD difference.

I

Integrity

Honesty in reporting, integrity in decisions, integrity in how we treat people.

V

Value

Operational and commercial discipline. We respect the value our work creates.

E

Environment

Care for the environment and the communities where we operate.

Everyday life

What an AD day looks like.

On the rig, mornings start with safety briefings and toolbox talks. Crews work shifts that depend on each other. Decisions are made in real time and reviewed at the end of each rotation.

At HQ, work is paced around operations. Reviews, planning sessions, and cross-function coordination drive the day.

Leadership & trust

How AD leads.

Visible leadership

Leaders show up in the field. Crews see the decision makers, not just hear about them.

Clear standards

Expectations are explicit. Performance is measured against the standard, not against opinion.

Coaching culture

Feedback is regular and useful. Promotion follows performance and readiness.
