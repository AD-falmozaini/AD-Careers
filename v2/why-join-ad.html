Our promise

Arabian Drilling offers careers for people who want to work safely, grow through experience, and contribute to reliable energy operations across Saudi Arabia.

In return, we expect commitment to safety, teamwork, technical excellence, integrity, and reliable performance.

The two-way deal

What we offer. What we expect.

Every career decision sits on this. We are direct about both sides.

What AD offers

  • A safety-first work culture and clear expectations around performance.
  • Hands-on learning through real operational challenges and exposure.
  • Career paths across onshore, offshore, HSE, maintenance, engineering, supply chain, corporate, and early careers.
  • Work connected to Saudi Arabia’s energy sector and local talent development.
  • Team-based environments where communication, trust, and reliability matter.
  • Training, mentoring, and the responsibility that comes with growth.

What AD expects

  • Commitment to safety, teamwork, integrity, and technical discipline.
  • Care for people, assets, customers, communities, and the environment.
  • Clear communication, practical problem solving, and reliable follow-through.
  • Ownership of your development and readiness for the role you signed up for.
  • Pride in the work and respect for the people you work with.
Six pillars

Built on six career pillars.

Each pillar sits behind every role at AD, regardless of function or seniority.

01

Safety First

Safety is the foundation of every job, every site, and every decision. We never compromise.

02

Growth Through Experience

Careers develop through training, mentoring, feedback, and exposure to real operational challenges.

03

Technical Excellence

Disciplined, skilled people keep complex drilling operations running reliably.

04

Purpose & National Contribution

Our employees support Saudi Arabia’s energy sector and local talent development.

05

Teamwork & Connection

Performance depends on trust, communication, leadership, and collaboration.

06

Wellbeing & Resilience

Drilling work demands focus, discipline, and readiness. We back the conditions for it.

Is AD right for you?

AD suits people who:

  • Take safety seriously as part of their identity, not as a checklist item.
  • Want to learn by doing, not just by studying.
  • Are willing to work in demanding environments and rotation schedules where the role requires it.
  • Care about doing the work properly, not just quickly.
  • Want to build a long-term career, not just a short stint.
