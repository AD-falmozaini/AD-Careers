How hiring works

Hiring & Application Process.

Transparent, fair, and consistent. Here is what to expect when you apply for a role at Arabian Drilling.

Home›Hiring process
Step 01

Search & Apply

Search jobs and submit your application through the AD Careers portal.

Step 02

Application Review

Talent Acquisition reviews applications against role requirements.

Step 03

Screening

A short screening conversation with TA to discuss your background.

Step 04

Interview

Technical and behavioral interviews with the hiring manager and team.

Step 05

Assessment

Role-specific assessment where applicable. Could be technical, case, or practical.

Step 06

Offer

If successful, an offer follows, including comp details and start date.

Step 07

Onboarding

HSE induction, role orientation, and the start of your AD career.

How AD assesses fit

We evaluate technical competence, safety mindset, integrity, communication, and the ability to work in AD’s operational environment. Different roles weight these differently. Honesty matters.

How to prepare

Know your CV. Bring real examples. Be honest about what you have done and what you are still learning. Ask questions about the role and the team.

What AD values

Reliability, technical skill, safety leadership, and willingness to grow. We hire for the long term.

Transparency

We commit to clear communication at each stage. If you are not selected, you will be told. If you progress, you will know what comes next.

Recruitment fraud warning

AD never asks for payment from candidates and never makes job offers through unofficial channels. Read more on our recruiting fraud page.
