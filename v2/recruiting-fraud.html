Important

Recruiting Fraud Warning.

Protect yourself from fake job offers, unofficial messages, and recruitment payment scams falsely using Arabian Drilling’s name.

Home›Recruiting fraud

Arabian Drilling never asks candidates to pay for jobs.

If anyone asks you for payment, visa fees, training fees, or processing fees in connection with an AD job, it is a scam. Do not pay.

How AD recruitment actually works

  • All AD job openings are posted on the AD Careers portal.
  • Communication comes from official Arabian Drilling email domains.
  • AD does not request payment of any kind from candidates.
  • Offers are made in writing through the AD Careers portal or directly by Talent Acquisition.
  • AD does not use unofficial recruiters or third-party agents to demand payment from candidates.

Common scam signals

  • Job offer received without an interview.
  • Request for visa, training, or administrative fees.
  • Communication from personal email addresses, not company domains.
  • Urgency and pressure to pay quickly.
  • Unprofessional grammar, formatting, or branding.

Report suspicious activity

If you believe you have been contacted by someone falsely representing Arabian Drilling, do not respond, do not pay, and report it to AD Talent Acquisition through the AD Careers portal contact channels.
