Privacy

Candidate Privacy Notice.

How Arabian Drilling collects, processes, and protects candidate data during the recruitment process.

Home›Candidate privacy

What data we collect

When you apply for a role or join the Talent Community, AD collects information you provide directly, such as your name, contact details, CV, employment history, education, and the role(s) you are interested in. Some information may also be collected through assessments and interviews.

How we use your data

AD processes candidate data to assess applications, communicate with you about opportunities, conduct interviews and assessments, perform pre-employment checks where applicable, and manage Talent Community alerts. Where applicable, data is also used to comply with Saudi labor and regulatory requirements.

Lawful basis and PDPL

AD processes candidate data in accordance with the Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL). We rely on your consent, legitimate interest, and legal obligation where applicable.

Retention

Candidate data is retained for the duration needed to consider applications and to comply with applicable law. Talent Community profiles are retained while you remain interested. You can request deletion at any time.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal data. You also have the right to withdraw consent for Talent Community communication at any time.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact AD Talent Acquisition through the AD Careers portal. Specific contact channels and the full policy text will be confirmed by AD Legal and Communications before publication.
