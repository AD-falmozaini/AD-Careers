Why AD for experienced talent

Real scale. Real responsibility.

AD operates the largest onshore drilling fleet in the Kingdom and a growing offshore fleet. Experienced hires here run real operations, lead real teams, and make decisions that matter to the energy sector.

What we offer

  • Operating-scale experience few employers can match in the Kingdom.
  • Real authority and accountability within a clear DOA framework.
  • Modern enterprise systems and digital tooling.
  • Career paths with cross-entity and international exposure.

What we expect

  • Mastery of your discipline, applied in an operational setting.
  • Coaching and capability building of younger AD talent.
  • Discipline around safety, quality, and operational standards.
  • Pride in long-term contribution, not short-term resume points.

Arabian Drilling is the largest onshore drilling contractor in Saudi Arabia. Operating across AD and OFSAT entities.
