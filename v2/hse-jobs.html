HSE Jobs at Arabian Drilling.

HSE is not a side function at AD. It is how we operate. Our HSE professionals work on the rig floor, on offshore platforms, and at the corporate level.

Role in our mission

HSE professionals at AD do real work in the field.

Toolbox talks, audits, incident investigations, permit-to-work, drills, coaching. Our HSE function is embedded in operations, not running on the side.

What we offer in HSE

  • Real influence on operational safety culture across the AD fleet.
  • Field exposure on onshore and offshore assets.
  • Training and certification support where the role requires.
  • Career paths from field officer through senior HSE leadership.

What we expect in HSE

  • Visible field presence and the willingness to engage crews directly.
  • Strong technical and regulatory knowledge.
  • Coaching skills that build crew habits, not paperwork compliance.
  • Integrity in incident reporting and root cause discipline.

Typical roles

HSE Officer

Field HSE leadership on onshore or offshore assets.

HSE Advisor

Senior field role with mentoring and audit responsibility.

HSE Manager

Asset or fleet-level HSE leadership with crew, contractor, and client responsibility.

Occupational Health Specialist

Health programs, medical readiness, and wellbeing for field crews.

Environmental Specialist

Environmental compliance, monitoring, and reporting across operations.

Emergency Response Specialist

Drills, emergency response planning, and crisis preparedness.

Certifications

NEBOSH IGC, IOSH Managing Safely, OPITO BOSIET, and ISO 45001 are valued. Role requirements vary by job posting.

Skills

Field presence, clear communication in Arabic and English, technical knowledge, and the credibility to challenge unsafe behavior at any level.

Arabian Drilling is the largest onshore drilling contractor in Saudi Arabia. Operating across AD and OFSAT entities.
