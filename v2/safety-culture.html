Safety first

Safety Culture.

Safety is how we work. AD employees protect themselves, their teams, the assets they operate, and the environment they work in.

Home›Life at AD›Safety culture
Shared responsibility

Everyone owns safety.

Safety at AD is not delegated to a function. Drillers, supervisors, HSE officers, mechanics, supply chain, and corporate teams share responsibility for safe operations.

What we provide

  • HSE training and certification aligned with industry standards.
  • PPE, safety equipment, and visible HSE leadership on every site.
  • Permit-to-work, risk assessment, and emergency response programs.
  • Clear procedures and the right to stop unsafe work.

What we expect

  • Full compliance with site rules, PPE, and procedures.
  • Speaking up when something looks unsafe. Always.
  • Honest incident and near-miss reporting.
  • Care for colleagues, contractors, and the environment.

Risk awareness

Risk assessment is built into the daily rhythm of every operation.

Field readiness

Drills, emergency response, and crew readiness reviewed continuously.

Wellbeing

Physical, mental, and operational readiness are part of safety, not separate from it.
