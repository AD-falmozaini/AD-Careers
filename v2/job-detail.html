HSE Officer — Offshore

Support safe drilling operations across the Arabian Gulf. Drive HSE awareness, incident prevention, and field readiness on offshore assets.

Apply now →

About the role

The HSE Officer (Offshore) supports the offshore HSE program across AD's jackup operations. You will work alongside rig crews, supervisors, and contractors to keep people safe and operations compliant with AD standards and Saudi regulatory requirements.

This is a hands-on field role. You will be on the rig, conducting safety inspections, leading toolbox talks, supporting incident investigations, and helping crews build daily safety habits.

What you'll do

  • Lead daily safety briefings, toolbox talks, and pre-job risk assessments.
  • Conduct site inspections, audits, and observations across rig operations.
  • Support incident reporting, root cause analysis, and lessons learned distribution.
  • Coach crew members on PPE, lifting operations, working at height, and confined space safety.
  • Maintain HSE documentation, permit-to-work compliance, and emergency drill readiness.
  • Partner with rig supervisors, client representatives, and the offshore HSE manager.

What you bring

  • Bachelor's degree or technical diploma in occupational safety, engineering, or related field.
  • 5+ years' HSE experience in oil & gas, ideally offshore drilling or marine operations.
  • NEBOSH IGC, IOSH Managing Safely, or equivalent. OPITO BOSIET preferred for offshore.
  • Strong field presence and the ability to coach crews respectfully and clearly.
  • Comfortable with rotation schedules and offshore living conditions.
  • Arabic and English communication skills.

Why join AD

You will work on critical assets in one of the world's most active drilling markets. AD operates the largest onshore rig fleet in Saudi Arabia and a growing offshore presence. Our HSE function is central to how we operate, not a function on the side.

"Safety here is not a poster on the wall. It's how the driller, the floorman, the engineer, and the HSE officer talk to each other on the rig floor every shift."
Offshore HSE Manager · Arabian Drilling
