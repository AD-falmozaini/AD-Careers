Ethical Use of AI in Recruitment.

Guidance for responsible, honest use of AI tools during the Arabian Drilling recruitment process.

Home›Ethical AI

What is acceptable

  • Using AI tools to research AD and the role you are applying for.
  • Using AI to help structure or proofread your CV and cover letter, with your own content and experience.
  • Using AI for interview practice with role-relevant questions.

What is not acceptable

  • Using AI to fabricate experience, qualifications, or certifications on your CV.
  • Using AI to generate responses during a live interview or live assessment.
  • Using AI to misrepresent your identity, work history, or technical skills.

Why this matters

AD operations are safety-critical. Hiring decisions are based on real competence. Misrepresentation discovered at any stage can result in withdrawal of an offer or termination of employment.

How AD uses AI in recruitment

Where AI tools are used by AD in the recruitment process, decisions about candidates are made by trained AD recruiters and hiring managers. AI may support sorting, scheduling, and search but does not replace human evaluation.

Arabian Drilling is the largest onshore drilling contractor in Saudi Arabia. Operating across AD and OFSAT entities.
