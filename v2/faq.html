Careers FAQ.

Answers to common questions about applying, interviewing, profiles, job alerts, and life at AD.

Home›FAQ

Applying

How do I apply for a job at Arabian Drilling?
Search jobs through the AD Careers portal, create a candidate profile, and submit your application against the role you are interested in.
Can I apply for more than one role?
Yes. You can apply for multiple roles that match your qualifications. We recommend tailoring your CV to each role.
Do you accept walk-in CVs?
No. All applications must be submitted through the AD Careers portal so we maintain a fair and auditable process.

Your profile and alerts

Why should I create a candidate profile?
A profile lets you save jobs, track applications, set up job alerts, and apply faster to future roles.
How do I set up job alerts?
From your profile, set your interests, locations, and job areas. Alerts will be sent when matching roles are posted.

Interviews and assessment

What does the interview process look like?
A screening conversation with Talent Acquisition, technical and behavioral interviews with the hiring manager and team, and where applicable a practical or technical assessment. The full flow is on the hiring process page.
How should I prepare?
Know your CV, bring concrete examples of past work, and be honest about what you have done and what you are still learning. Ask questions.

Working at AD

What are AD’s working conditions like?
Field roles often involve rotation schedules and demanding operational environments. Corporate roles are based at AD HQ in Al Khobar. Specific conditions vary by role.
What benefits do you offer?
Benefits include healthcare, learning, family-related leave, and where applicable rotation support. Specific benefits vary by role, level, entity, and location. Talent Acquisition will share role-specific details.
How does career growth work?
Career paths vary by function, experience, and business needs. AD’s operational ladders are well defined and most senior field roles are filled internally.

Recruitment fraud

How do I avoid recruitment scams?
AD never asks candidates for payment. Job offers are made only through official AD channels. Read more on the recruiting fraud page.
