Maintenance & Engineering Jobs.

AD’s maintenance and engineering teams keep the fleet running. Mechanical, electrical, reliability, and drilling engineering work across onshore and offshore assets.

Role in our mission

Reliability is a daily decision, not a one-off project.

Mechanics, electricians, instrumentation techs, and drilling engineers protect AD’s uptime. Their work decides whether a rig is on or off.

What we offer

  • Hands-on work on complex, high-value drilling equipment.
  • Cross-functional exposure between drilling, HSE, and supply chain.
  • Technical training and OEM support for critical systems.
  • Career paths from technician through senior engineering and management.

What we expect

  • Discipline in preventive maintenance and reliability programs.
  • Sharp diagnostic skills and the patience to do the work properly.
  • Safety leadership during isolation, lockout, and lifting operations.
  • Strong handover and documentation habits.

Typical roles

Rig Mechanic

Mechanical reliability of drilling and rig equipment.

Rig Electrician

Electrical systems, drives, and rig power.

Instrumentation Technician

Drilling instrumentation, control systems, and monitoring.

Drilling Engineer

Drilling program design, planning, and field engineering support.

Reliability Engineer

Fleet-level reliability programs and asset performance improvement.

Maintenance Supervisor

Lead the maintenance crew across rig sites or workshop operations.

Arabian Drilling is the largest onshore drilling contractor in Saudi Arabia. Operating across AD and OFSAT entities.
