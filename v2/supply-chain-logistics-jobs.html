Role in our mission

Without supply chain, the rig stops.

Procurement teams, warehouses, and logistics coordinators keep critical spares, consumables, and crew change movements aligned with operations.

What we offer

  • Exposure to oilfield procurement, supplier management, and inventory control.
  • Work at field sites and at AD HQ in Al Khobar.
  • Cross-functional partnership with operations, maintenance, and finance.
  • Career paths from coordinator through category management and supply chain leadership.

What we expect

  • Discipline around process, documentation, and audit trail.
  • Commercial judgment balanced with operational urgency.
  • Care for safety in warehouse and logistics environments.
  • Reliable follow-through across stakeholders.

Typical roles

Procurement Specialist

Sourcing, tendering, and supplier negotiation for rig and corporate categories.

Warehouse Coordinator

Inventory accuracy, dispatch, and warehouse operations.

Logistics Coordinator

Crew change, equipment movement, and transport planning.

Category Manager

Strategic sourcing across drilling, maintenance, or services categories.

Supply Chain Analyst

Reporting, vendor performance, and supply chain analytics.

Materials Controller

Materials planning across rigs, workshops, and central warehouse.
