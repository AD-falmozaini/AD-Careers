Rewards

Benefits & Rewards.

We support our people through competitive rewards, wellbeing programs, learning, and career growth. Benefits may vary by role and location.

Home›Life at AD›Benefits

Health & wellbeing

Medical, wellbeing programs, and access to occupational health support.

Competitive pay

Compensation aligned with role, market, and performance.

Time off

Annual leave, rotation cycles where applicable, and statutory leave entitlements.

Learning

Technical training, leadership development, and tuition support where the role qualifies.

Family support

Family-related leave and benefits aligned with Saudi labor law.

Field rotation support

Travel, accommodation, and rotation support for field-based roles.

Important note

Specific benefit details, eligibility, and amounts vary by role, level, entity (AD or OFSAT), and location. Talent Acquisition will share role-specific information during the hiring process.
