Internship Program.

Learn by doing through practical experience, mentoring, and exposure to technical and corporate work.

Why intern with AD

Real work for university students.

AD internships place students inside live functions and operations. You will sit with engineers, HSE professionals, supply chain specialists, or corporate teams and contribute to current projects.

Program details should be confirmed by Talent Acquisition. Eligibility and disciplines vary by intake.

What we offer interns

  • Structured project work with a defined deliverable.
  • A line manager and a mentor who own your experience.
  • Exposure to AD operations and how the company works.
  • Networking across the AD and OFSAT entities.

What we expect of interns

  • Curiosity, ownership, and reliability through the placement.
  • Professional behavior in AD offices and field sites.
  • Compliance with safety briefings and site rules at all times.
  • A written project deliverable at the end of the program.

How to apply

Watch the AD Careers job feed for open internship requisitions. Saudi university students at relevant disciplines should also create a profile and join the talent community.
