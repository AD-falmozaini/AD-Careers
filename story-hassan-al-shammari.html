Questions and answers

Hassan discusses field readiness and learning.

What advice do you give new joiners?

Learn the procedure, respect the permit process, and ask when something is not clear.

What builds confidence?

Repeating the basics correctly and knowing the crew is communicating openly.

What makes a safe crew?

People who speak early, check each other, and treat every task seriously.

How do you use communication during a shift?

I confirm what I heard, raise concerns early, and make sure the next person understands the status.

What did you learn from experienced crews?

They taught me that discipline in simple routines prevents bigger issues later.

What keeps you motivated?

Seeing the team complete work safely and knowing everyone contributed.
