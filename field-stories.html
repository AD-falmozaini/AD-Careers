Questions and answers

An interview-style look at Fahad’s work, growth, and advice for new field employees.

How did your career at AD begin?

I started by learning the rhythm of the rig and the importance of preparation before every task. The senior crew members helped me understand that good field work starts before the operation begins.

What does a strong day in the field look like?

A strong day is when everyone understands the plan, checks the equipment, communicates early, and finishes the job safely. When the crew is aligned, the work becomes smoother and more reliable.

What helped you grow?

Feedback from supervisors and experienced colleagues helped me improve. I learned to ask questions, stay alert, and take ownership of the small details that protect people and equipment.

What advice would you give new employees?

Respect the procedure, listen to your crew, and do not rush your learning. In the field, your reputation is built through reliability, safety behavior, and teamwork.
