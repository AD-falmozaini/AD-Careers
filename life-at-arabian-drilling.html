Our values

Built around people, safety, and national contribution

STRIVE

Arabian Drilling describes its high-performance culture through the STRIVE value model. For candidates, that means joining a workplace where safe work, reliable execution, integrity, teamwork, environmental responsibility, and value creation are part of the same daily standard.

Culture model

How STRIVE shows up at work

These values come from Arabian Drilling’s corporate workplace culture and have been translated here for candidates exploring careers.

S

Safety

Protecting people, contractors, customers, communities, assets, and the environment is central to how AD works.

T

Teamwork

Field, offshore, workshop, and office teams coordinate across disciplines to keep operations moving.

R

Reliability

Employees contribute to dependable drilling performance through preparation, follow-through, and clear communication.

I

Integrity

Trust, accountability, compliance, and honest conduct guide decisions with candidates, colleagues, and partners.

V

Value

AD teams focus on performance, quality, and practical improvement that supports customers and the wider economy.

E

Environment

Environmental responsibility is connected to safe operations, efficient work, and long-term sustainability commitments.

Everyday experience

What it feels like to work at AD

Everyday work varies by role, but the employee experience is grounded in safety, trust, communication, and disciplined execution.

Field and offshore teamwork

Operational teams rely on clear handovers, readiness, respect for procedures, and care for colleagues in demanding environments.

Corporate support with purpose

Office teams support the people, systems, governance, and decisions that keep drilling operations reliable.

Leadership and trust

Employees are expected to communicate clearly, act with integrity, and build trust across functions and backgrounds.

Our people

Powered by a multinational workforce

Arabian Drilling’s corporate site describes a workforce of 6000+ employees across more than 30 nationalities. The careers experience should help candidates see where they can contribute, grow capability, and join teams that care about safety, development, and performance.
