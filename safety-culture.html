Safety Culture

Safety is the foundation of every job, every site, and every decision. AD employees protect themselves, their teams, assets, communities, and the environment.

Safety culture

Make safe behavior visible in every role and every conversation.

Shared responsibility

Safe performance starts before the task begins

Safety culture is not a slogan. It is the daily discipline of planning work, identifying risk, speaking up, following procedures, and caring for the people around you.

What AD provides

A safety-first work environment, procedures, HSE expectations, communication routines, and the operational discipline needed for reliable performance.

What AD expects

Employees must take responsibility for their own safety and the safety of their team, stop and think before work, and follow role-specific procedures.

Why it matters

Safe work protects people, supports wellbeing and readiness, reduces operational risk, and helps AD deliver reliable energy services.

Field readiness

Risk awareness, wellbeing, and performance belong together

Role requirements vary by posting, but safety-minded candidates should be ready to show practical judgment, communication, and care for others.
