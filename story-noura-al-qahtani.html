Questions and answers

Noura shares what helped her move from graduate learning into confident contribution.

What surprised you in your first months?

I expected technical work, but I also learned how important communication is.

How did you build credibility?

I prepared before meetings, documented what I learned, and followed up on feedback.

Who supported your development?

Managers, mentors, and technical colleagues all played a role.

How did assignments help you learn?

Each assignment connected a technical concept to a real business or operational need.

What skill became more important than expected?

Explaining technical information clearly and asking the right questions before making assumptions.

What advice would you give graduates?

Stay curious, ask for feedback, and treat every assignment as a chance to understand the business better.
