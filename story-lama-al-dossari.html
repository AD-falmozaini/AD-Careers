Questions and answers

Lama shares her graduate experience in HR.

What helped you settle in?

Clear feedback, supportive colleagues, and seeing how my work connected to real employee needs.

What did you learn about HR?

HR is close to the business because every process affects employees and managers.

How did you build confidence?

By preparing carefully, asking questions, and following through on assigned actions.

What makes HR work meaningful?

It connects policy, support, and daily employee experience in a very practical way.

How do you learn from different teams?

I listen to the context behind each request so I understand what people really need.

What advice would you share?

Understand the people behind the process. That makes the work more meaningful.
