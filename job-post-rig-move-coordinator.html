Rig Move Coordinator

Job Location

Rigs / Field

Reports to

Job Purpose

Plans rig moves, transport timing, vendors, and field readiness. This talent-pool posting helps Arabian Drilling identify qualified candidates for current and future logistics opportunities.

Key Tasks & Responsibilities

  • Rig moves, transport timing, and route readiness
  • Materials, marine support, and vendor coordination
  • Lifting and movement planning with safe execution
  • Documentation, permits, and field communication

Total Years of Experience (Minimum)

3+ years in logistics, transport, lifting, marine, or rig-move support is preferred.

Relevant Experience

Candidates should be comfortable working in Arabian Drilling's disciplined, safety-first environment, with clear communication, teamwork, accountability, and practical follow-through.

Education and Qualifications

Diploma or high school qualification with relevant logistics or operations experience is preferred.

Certifications

Lifting, defensive driving, marine, or logistics certification may be required by role.

General Guideline and Disclaimer

The employee must adhere to applicable policies, processes, procedures, instructions, and controls so that work is carried out in a consistent, best-in-class, safe, secure, and sustainable manner. This prototype summarizes the typical nature and level of the role; final responsibilities, qualifications, location, rotation, and requisition data must be confirmed in SuccessFactors before launch.
