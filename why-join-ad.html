The AD employee promise

Work safely, grow continuously, and contribute with purpose

Arabian Drilling offers careers for people who want to work safely, grow through experience, and contribute to reliable energy operations across Saudi Arabia. In return, AD expects commitment to safety, teamwork, technical excellence, integrity, and performance.

What AD offers

Opportunities to learn, contribute, and build capability in a safety-first environment across field, offshore, maintenance, HSE, supply chain, corporate, and early-career work. Talent pool fit varies by function, experience, and business needs.

What AD expects

Visible commitment to safe work, disciplined execution, clear communication, integrity, care for others, and continuous improvement in every role and work setting.
