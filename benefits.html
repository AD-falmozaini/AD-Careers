Employee support

Benefits organized around the way people work.

Arabian Drilling benefits content should help candidates understand the practical support behind safe work, field readiness, career growth, and wellbeing. Final policy details, eligibility, and allowances should be confirmed by Arabian Drilling before launch.

Candidate note

Eligibility, coverage, allowances, and program availability may vary by role, grade, location, contract type, and company policy.

01

Rewards and recognition

Rewards should connect to contribution, performance, role responsibility, and approved field or rotation allowances where applicable.

02

Health, wellbeing, and resilience

Support should cover medical and site readiness, responsible work habits, wellbeing, and family support where approved by policy.

03

Career growth

Employees can build capability through technical learning, certification support where approved, mentoring, and movement across functions.

04

Work environment support

Field, offshore, workshop, and office teams need safety equipment, clear standards, communication, teamwork, and readiness support.

Rotation and site readiness

For field and offshore roles, explain rotation preparation, mobilization documents, travel or camp expectations, and safety readiness checks where applicable.

Medical and safety readiness

Show how medical checks, PPE, safety orientation, and site requirements help employees arrive prepared for demanding operational environments.

Learning and certification support

Connect benefits to role-based training, technical certification, compliance learning, mentoring, and career movement across Arabian Drilling functions.

Field and role allowances

Where confirmed by policy, explain allowances, schedules, and work-pattern support in plain language so candidates know what to ask before accepting an offer.

Family and wellbeing support

Use approved wording to describe practical wellbeing support, family considerations, resilience, and responsible work habits for long-term performance.

Career movement

Help candidates see how experience in rigs, yards, workshops, offices, and support teams can open adjacent paths over time.

What AD offers

Support for safe contribution

AD offers candidates the opportunity to contribute to reliable operations, build skills, and work with teams that value safety, discipline, and practical problem solving. Benefits may vary by role, location, grade, and policy.
