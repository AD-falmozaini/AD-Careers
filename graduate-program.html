Graduate development

Build foundations for a long-term energy career

The graduate experience should help early-career talent understand the business, build technical confidence, learn safe work habits, and contribute through supervised assignments.

Who can apply

Early-career candidates should be ready to show their education, major, achievements, English capability, and interest in energy, operations, engineering, business, or support functions.

What you experience

Graduates can expect onboarding, practical assignments, mentoring, feedback, and exposure to the teams that keep Arabian Drilling operations safe and reliable.

What AD expects

Bring curiosity, safety awareness, accountability, teamwork, technical discipline, and willingness to learn from experienced supervisors and specialists.
