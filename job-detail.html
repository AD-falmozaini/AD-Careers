Role summary

Make safety visible every day

The HSE Officer supports safe, compliant, and environmentally responsible drilling operations by conducting inspections, advising field teams, supporting incident prevention, and reinforcing Arabian Drilling’s safety-first culture.

Arabian Drilling context

Driving value, delivering excellence

Arabian Drilling describes its work as industry-leading drilling services backed by a culture of quality and safety. This role connects directly to that promise by helping field teams keep safety, reliability, and environmental responsibility at the center of daily operations.

Since 1964 A long operating heritage across oil and gas drilling in Saudi Arabia.
6000+ workforce A multinational team with broad operational and technical experience.
STRIVE values Safety, Teamwork, Reliability, Integrity, Value, and Environment.

What you’ll do

  • Conduct HSE inspections, audits, and site observations across assigned work areas.
  • Coach employees and contractors on safe work practices, permit-to-work discipline, and field risk controls.
  • Support incident reporting, investigation, corrective actions, and lessons learned.
  • Monitor compliance with HSE procedures, client requirements, and applicable regulatory expectations.
  • Participate in toolbox talks, risk assessments, emergency drills, and safety campaigns.

What you bring

  • Relevant HSE qualification and oil and gas field experience.
  • Strong understanding of risk assessment, hazard observation, and incident prevention.
  • Clear communication, reporting, coaching, and teamwork skills.
  • Certifications such as NEBOSH, IOSH, OSHA, or equivalent are preferred.

Where you’ll work

This prototype role is listed for Saudi Arabia and may support field, rig, workshop, or operational locations depending on business need. Final work location, rotation, and travel expectations should be confirmed by Talent Acquisition.

What success looks like

Field confidence

Teams understand the risk controls needed to complete work safely and reliably.

Action follow-through

Observations, audits, and incident actions are documented and closed with care.

STRIVE in practice

Safety, Teamwork, Reliability, Integrity, Value, and Environment are visible in daily decisions.
