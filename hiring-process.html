What guides hiring

Built around people, safety, and national contribution

S.T.R.I.V.E For Excellence

Safety. Safety-first decisions guide role requirements, field readiness, interviews, and onboarding.

Teamwork. Candidates, recruiters, hiring teams, and operations stay aligned through the process.

Reliability. Clear steps, dependable communication, and practical expectations build trust with candidates.

Integrity. Fair evaluation and recruitment fraud protection help candidates apply through official channels.

Value. Hiring focuses on capability, readiness, contribution, and long-term fit with AD teams.

Environment. Candidates join teams that work responsibly and protect people, assets, and operating environments.

Common questions

Hiring process FAQ

Answers should stay neutral where role-specific policy details need confirmation from Talent Acquisition.

How do I apply for a role?

Search open jobs, select a role that matches your experience, and use the apply CTA on the job detail page. The final application flow should connect to AD’s ATS.

Will every role follow the same process?

The core journey is similar, but field, offshore, technical, HSE, and corporate roles may include different interviews, checks, or assessments.

Can I join if there is no suitable role today?

Yes. Join the Talent Community to share your interests and receive future job alerts when matching opportunities become available.
