Questions and answers

Omar shares how he connects engineering to operations.

What advice do you give graduates?

Take notes, ask why, and use every assignment to understand the operation from another angle.

What made the work real?

Site visits helped me see the effect of engineering choices on rig performance.

What skill mattered most?

Learning to plan carefully and communicate technical details simply.

How do you connect planning to safety?

Good planning reduces surprises, and fewer surprises help teams work with better control.

What did maintenance teach you about reliability?

Reliability depends on discipline before failure happens, not only response after something breaks.

What keeps you growing?

Each assignment shows me another part of how reliability supports safe operations.
