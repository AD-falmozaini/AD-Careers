Job Purpose

Creates communication plans, internal messages, and stakeholder updates. This talent-pool posting helps Arabian Drilling identify qualified candidates for current and future communication opportunities.

Key Tasks & Responsibilities

  • Communication plans, internal messages, and stakeholder updates
  • Events logistics, schedules, and participant communication
  • Content development for campaigns and digital channels
  • Brand-aligned messaging for employee and candidate audiences

Total Years of Experience (Minimum)

Experience in internal communication, events, content, digital channels, or stakeholder engagement is preferred.

Relevant Experience

Candidates should be comfortable working in Arabian Drilling's disciplined, safety-first environment, with clear communication, teamwork, accountability, and practical follow-through.

Education and Qualifications

Bachelor's degree in communications, marketing, media, business, or a related discipline is preferred.

Certifications

Communication, events, content, or digital marketing certification is an advantage.
