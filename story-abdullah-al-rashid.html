Questions and answers

Abdullah shares how leaders support safety culture.

What makes a leader effective?

Consistency. Teams trust leaders who act the same way under pressure as they do in normal conditions.

How do you encourage speaking up?

By taking concerns seriously and showing that early reporting prevents bigger problems.

What is safety leadership?

Making expectations clear and helping people apply them during real work.

How do you make safety conversations practical?

I connect them to the actual task, the actual risk, and the decision the team needs to make.

What do you expect from supervisors?

They should listen, act quickly, and make sure the team sees that safety standards are real.

What advice would you share?

Listen carefully, act consistently, and make safety visible every day.
