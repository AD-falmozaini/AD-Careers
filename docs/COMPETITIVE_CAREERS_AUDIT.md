## What Winners Have In Common

1. They behave like employer-brand hubs, not only job boards.
2. They put job search high in the page and repeat the path to open roles.
3. They use authentic people, workplace, location, and role-context content.
4. They segment candidates by job family, location, career level, and interests.
5. They make job detail pages richer with role-specific context, not generic ATS text.
6. They use measurable conversion points: talent community, saved jobs, job alerts, event signups, and profile creation.
7. They keep navigation simple, with clear links to culture, benefits, locations, categories, and search.

## AD Careers Improvement Recommendations

### 1. Make Job Area Pages Feel Like Real Landing Pages

The AD job-area pages should each have a unique above-the-fold story: image, role promise, role-specific search, featured roles, career pathway, required capabilities, related culture content, and FAQs. Avoid repeating the same generic layout across onshore, offshore, HSE, maintenance, supply chain, and corporate.

Priority pages:
- Onshore Drilling Jobs
- Offshore Drilling Jobs
- HSE Jobs
- Maintenance & Engineering Jobs
- Supply Chain & Logistics Jobs
- Corporate Careers

### 2. Upgrade Job Details Into “Enhanced Job Descriptions”

Add scannable bands to the job detail page:
- Why this role matters
- Day-in-the-life responsibilities
- Safety and environment expectations
- Field/work pattern context
- Career path from this role
- Certifications and readiness
- Related roles in the same job family
- Apply, save job, and join talent community CTAs

This follows the pattern Rally highlighted for Delaware North: job descriptions enriched with images, video, career paths, role and location context.

### 3. Add Employee Storytelling

Create a reusable employee-story card pattern:
- Name / role / location
- Quote
- Job family tag
- Link to related jobs

For AD, this can start as neutral prototype content and later be replaced with approved HR/Comms stories. Use approved corporate imagery only.

### 4. Turn Life at AD Into a Content Hub

Life at AD should work like a gateway to:
- STRIVE values
- Workplace culture
- Safety culture
- Learning and development
- Benefits
- Saudization and inclusion
- Employee stories

Credicorp’s winning pattern is useful here: a careers site can house content, campaigns, and thought leadership, not just openings.

### 5. Improve Search and Job Discovery

Add a “jobs near you / roles by area” discovery module:
- Keyword
- Location
- Job area
- Career level
- Popular searches
- Recent jobs

For prototype purposes, this remains static; mark the integration points for SuccessFactors/ATS.

### 6. Make Talent Community More Conversion-Focused

Split forms into two clear journeys:
- New candidate registration
- Returning member sign-in

Keep the corporate contact-form visual style, but add progressive sections for preferences:
- Job areas
- Locations
- Career level
- Notification preferences
- CV upload placeholder

### 7. Add Measurement Notes To The Prototype

Document analytics events for:
- Search submission
- Filter use
- Job view
- Apply click
- Save job
- Talent community signup
- Profile login
- FAQ expansion

The Rally winners explicitly used analytics and optimization; AD should include the measurement plan in the prototype docs.

### 8. Tone Down Hover Effects

Award-winning sites use interaction to clarify, not overwhelm. For AD, card hover should be subtle:
- Slight lift or image reveal
- Clear text contrast
- Arrow/link movement
- No unreadable dark overlays
- No heavy color inversion on dense content cards

### 9. Add Candidate Trust Content Earlier

Bring fraud warning, privacy, accessibility, and hiring process support into related moments:
- On talent community forms
- On job detail pages
- Near apply CTAs
- In hiring process FAQs

### 10. Build Arabic Readiness Into The IA

Keep Arabic SEO terms and page equivalents documented. The structure should support mirrored Arabic pages later, especially for high-intent searches around drilling jobs, oil and gas jobs, internships, and graduate programs in Saudi Arabia.
