What AD offers and expects onshore

Onshore careers put candidates close to land rig operations, where safe execution, teamwork, technical readiness, and reliable follow-through shape daily performance.

  • What AD offersHands-on operational exposure, experienced field teams, and practical learning in land rig environments.
  • What AD expectsSafety discipline, readiness for field work, respect for procedures, clear handovers, and care for team performance.
  • Growth possibilitiesCareer paths may move through rig operations, maintenance, HSE, supervision, technical support, or planning roles.
