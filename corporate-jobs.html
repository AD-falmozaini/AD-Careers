Corporate careers

What AD offers and expects in this area

Corporate roles help translate AD’s business ambition into people services, systems, governance, communications, and decisions that support operations.

  • What AD offersExposure to an industrial business, cross-functional stakeholders, and work that supports field and offshore teams.
  • What AD expectsConfidentiality, service mindset, accurate work, business judgment, integrity, and clear communication.
  • Growth possibilitiesProgression may include specialist, analyst, project, functional leadership, or business partner paths.

Typical corporate roles

HR specialist, finance analyst, IT support analyst, legal advisor, communications specialist, administrator, business analyst, and project coordinator roles.

What helps you succeed

Business judgment, service mindset, confidentiality, digital fluency, stakeholder management, clear writing, and comfort working with operational colleagues.
