HSE careers

What AD offers and expects in HSE

HSE careers give candidates the chance to influence safe work, coach teams, improve operational discipline, and connect wellbeing to performance.

  • What AD offersWork that protects people, assets, communities, and the environment through visible safety leadership.
  • What AD expectsRisk awareness, prevention mindset, clear communication, field credibility, and commitment to safe performance.
  • Growth possibilitiesCareer paths may include site HSE, environmental roles, audit support, safety leadership, or specialist HSE programs.
What the work feels like

Trusted, visible, and close to the work

Strong HSE professionals combine technical knowledge with credibility in the field. They listen, coach, document clearly, and help people make safer choices under operational pressure.

Which certifications help for HSE jobs?

Requirements vary, but postings may prefer or require recognized safety qualifications such as NEBOSH, IOSH, OSHA, or equivalent experience.

Are HSE jobs only field based?

HSE roles may be field, office, workshop, offshore, or hybrid depending on the specific requisition and operational need.
