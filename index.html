Employee promise

What AD offers, and what AD expects

At AD, careers are built on a clear promise: opportunities to learn, contribute, and grow in a safety-first environment; in return, we expect teamwork, integrity, technical discipline, and commitment to performance.

Safety First

You will work in an environment where safe behavior, risk awareness, and care for others matter. You are expected to take responsibility for your own safety and the safety of your team.

Growth Through Experience

AD careers develop through training, hands-on exposure, mentoring, and real operational challenges. You are expected to stay curious and take ownership of your development.

Technical Excellence

You will work with experienced teams, critical assets, and high operational standards. You are expected to bring professionalism, discipline, and attention to detail.

Purpose and National Contribution

Your work contributes to reliable energy operations and national capability. You are expected to act with pride, responsibility, and long-term thinking.

Teamwork and Human Connection

You will work with people who rely on each other in demanding environments. You are expected to communicate clearly, support colleagues, and respect different backgrounds.

Wellbeing and Resilience

Drilling work requires focus and readiness. You will be part of a culture that values safe, responsible work, and you are expected to follow procedures and maintain role readiness.
