Offshore careers

What AD offers and expects offshore

Offshore careers connect technical discipline, safety readiness, marine coordination, and teamwork in a demanding operating environment.

  • What AD offersExposure to offshore operations, experienced crews, critical assets, and work where planning and safety decisions matter.
  • What AD expectsReadiness, clear communication, procedure discipline, care for others, and reliable follow-through.
  • Growth possibilitiesCareer paths may progress through technical specialist, crew leadership, HSE, marine support, or operations supervision roles.

Typical offshore roles

Offshore electrician, offshore mechanic, toolpusher, marine coordinator, HSE officer, maintenance technician, drilling supervisor, and logistics support roles.

What helps you succeed

Strong safety awareness, comfort with offshore procedures, technical reliability, documentation discipline, and ability to collaborate across rotating teams.
