Questions and answers

Reem discusses growth and leadership support.

How do you support growth?

By connecting feedback, learning, and opportunity so employees can act on a clear development path.

What makes development practical?

Employees need clear actions they can apply in their current role.

What should managers do well?

Have honest conversations and follow up on commitments.

How do you make career conversations useful?

I focus on specific strengths, specific gaps, and the next action the employee can take.

What does good feedback look like?

It is clear, timely, respectful, and connected to the work the employee actually does.

What motivates you?

Seeing people become more confident in their next step.
